Movement & Productivity

Exploring the relationship between daily physical activity and academic output — Feb 10 through Apr 2, 2026. Data is self-collected: activity metrics exported from Apple Health, study hours and assignments logged manually by the author.

Dual-Encoded Calendar Heatmap

Each cell is one day. Fill color encodes step count (lighter pink = more steps), and border thickness encodes assignments completed that day (thicker gold border = more assignments). The dual encoding lets you scan for days that are bright on both channels — high activity and high output — versus days where one dominates. Click any cell to pull up that day's full stats and highlight it on the line chart below.

Steps & Assignments Over Time

Daily step count (pink) and assignments completed (gold) plotted over time. The two series share an x-axis but use independent y-axes, so vertical alignment between the lines reflects same-day co-occurrence rather than identical magnitude. Watch for weeks where the lines rise and fall together versus weeks where they diverge — that's the mixed signal driving this project's open question.
